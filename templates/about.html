About This System

What It Does

Collects macro-financial streams (news, central banks, calendar, markets), stores them in SQLite, computes analytics, and serves a UI/API for monitoring.

Core Modules

scrapers, storage, analysis, routes, workers, static/templates.

Automation Scope

Automates ingestion, normalization, persistence, and dashboards. It does not replace discretionary portfolio management.

Known Limits

Some analytics are heuristic proxies, and live market quality depends on IBKR entitlements and connectivity.
